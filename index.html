About Me

My name is William Lowrimore. Originally from Memphis, TN, I have spent a majority of my professional life as an established musician. I have toured and recorded with various artists. On my down time I turned to instructing music theory to budding musicians of all ages. As my family tree is comprised of successful music artists and computer software "techies", it was only a matter of time before I lept into a world of software development. I recently transplanted my roots, just up the "Music Highway" to Nashville, TN. Here in Nashville, I decided to take that leap and enroll in Vanderbilt University's Coding Bootcamp. With 20+ years of experience in music theory mathematics, creative composition, and the ability to convey these teachings, I felt as though software development was the next stop on my journey. I am currently "deep diving" into HTML, CSS and Git as I work toward a Full Stack Developer Certification. Next stop...JavaScript. And from there...THE SKY'S THE LIMIT!
